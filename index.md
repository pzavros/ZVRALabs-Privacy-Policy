# Privacy Policy for Posture Buddy

**Effective date:** February 16, 2026  
**Owner/Controller:** Panagiotis Zavros  
**App:** Posture Buddy (`com.zavros.posturepal`)  
**Website/Repository:** https://github.com/pzavros/PostureBuddy  
**Jurisdiction:** Greece (EU)

This Privacy Policy explains how Posture Buddy handles information when you use the app.

## Data Collection

Posture Buddy does **not** collect account or signup data in-app (such as name, password, or user profile fields).

The app processes the following data:

1. **Local on-device app data (SharedPreferences):**
   - reminder interval
   - hold duration
   - quiet hours
   - haptics/theme settings
   - daily completions
   - hold counts
   - streaks
   - timestamps
   - coins/xp
   - unlocked cosmetics
   - achievement progress
   - hold state
   - notification preference
   - last tip

2. **Ad-related data (Google AdMob SDK):**
   - device/advertising identifiers
   - IP address
   - approximate location
   - ad interaction, performance, and diagnostic data (as processed by Google)

## How We Use Data

We use data to:
- run core posture reminder features,
- save your progress and settings on your device,
- show and measure in-app ads (banner, interstitial, rewarded), and
- send local reminder notifications.

## Data Sharing

We share data only as follows:
- **Google AdMob / Google** as an ad processor,
- **Google Play services** used by AdMob integration.

Other than the above, the app owner does **not** sell personal data.

## Tracking and Cookies

- The app does **not** use website cookies in-app.
- Mobile ad tracking identifiers may be used by AdMob for ad delivery and measurement.

## Permissions

Posture Buddy uses these Android permissions:
- `INTERNET`
- `POST_NOTIFICATIONS`

## Data Retention

- Local app data is retained until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
- Ad-related data is retained by Google according to Google/AdMob policies.

## Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Children’s Privacy

Posture Buddy is for a general audience and is **not** directed to children under 13. We do not intentionally collect children’s personal data.

## International Transfers

Posture Buddy is operated from Greece (EU). Data processed by Google AdMob/Google Play services may be processed internationally according to Google’s infrastructure and policies.

## Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA/CPRA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- access personal data,
- request correction,
- request deletion,
- request information about processing and sharing,
- object to or restrict certain processing where applicable, and
- not be discriminated against for exercising applicable privacy rights.

For deletion of local app data, you can clear app storage or uninstall the app.
